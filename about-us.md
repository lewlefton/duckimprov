The Decatur yoUth Comedy Krewe (aka D.U.C.K.) is a group of middle and high school students from the City of Decatur who perform both short form and long form improv. All performances are completely improvised. At every show, the cast creates highly entertaining characters and scenes based on audience suggestions. Each performance is unique and unpredictable, but they are all high energy, full of audience interaction and great fun. Audience members are even encouraged to "bring a prop" for use in the show! We're a locally grown, kid-driven, community troupe, and you have to check us out!\*

#### \*No, seriously, I know you have things to do, but you actually have to.It's like a law or something. Someone we know got in trouble for not doing it.
