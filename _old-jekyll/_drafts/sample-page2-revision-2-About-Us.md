<span style="font-size: small;">The Decatur yoUth Comedy Krewe (aka D.U.C.K.) is a group of middle and high school students from the City of Decatur who perform both short form and long form improv.</span>

<span style="font-size: small;">All performances are completely improvised. At every show, the cast creates highly entertaining characters and scenes based on audience suggestions. Each performance is unique and unpredictable, but they are all high energy, full of audience interaction and great fun. Audience members are even encouraged to "bring a prop" for use in the show!</span>

<span style="font-size: small;">We're a locally grown, kid-driven, community troupe, and </span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">**you have to check us out**</span></span><span style="font-size: small;">!\*</span>

<span style="font-size: x-small;">\*No, seriously, I know you have things to do, but you actually have to.It's like a law or something. Someone we know got in trouble for not doing it.</span>
